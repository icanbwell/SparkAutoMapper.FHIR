AdverseEvent adverseevent.xsd

Actual or potential/avoided event causing unintended physical injury

resulting from or contributed to by medical care, a research study or other healthcare setting factors that requires additional monitoring, treatment, or hospitalization, or that results in death.

If the element is present, it must have either a @value, an @id, or extensions
